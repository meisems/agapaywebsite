For Patients

Patient Information

Preparation is key to accurate lab results. Here is what you need to know before visiting us.

Fasting Guidelines

Tests like FBS and Lipid Profile require strict fasting (no food or drinks except water). FBS requires 8-10 hours, while Lipid Profile requires 10-12 hours of fasting.

Specimen Collection

For urine tests, a "midstream clean catch" is required (discard the first few drops, collect the middle portion). The first morning urine is always preferred.

Releasing of Results

Results can be claimed physically at the clinic. For your convenience, official results can also be sent directly to your email or your physician's email upon request and verification.
