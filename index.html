Patient Guidelines

What to Expect

Your visit to Agapay Clinical Laboratory is designed to be safe, efficient, and comfortable from start to finish.

Walk-ins Welcome

No appointment needed for routine tests. Walk-ins are accommodated. Appointments preferred for specialized panels and health packages.

Patient Registration

Proceed to the reception area to register and receive assistance. The receptionist will answer questions about test options and turnaround times.

Safety & Order

Patients and companions must remain in the waiting area and specimen collection zones to maintain order and prevent contamination.

Confidentiality Assured

All patient information is strictly confidential and protected under the Philippine Data Privacy Act and DOH regulations.

Estimated Turnaround Times

Most routine results are available the same day. Turnaround time may vary depending on specimen type and time of receipt.
