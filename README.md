# Agapay Clinical Laboratory Website

A modern, professional, and fully responsive single-page website for **Agapay Clinical Laboratory**, a DOH-licensed clinical laboratory located in Dasmariñas City, Cavite, Philippines.

![Agapay Clinical Laboratory](https://github.com/meisems/agapaywebsite/blob/main/images/agapaycl.jpg)

## ✨ Features

- **Clean & Professional Design** with medical/laboratory aesthetic (green & gold color scheme)
- **Fully Responsive** – optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Smooth Animations** and scroll-triggered reveals
- **Interactive Test Menu** with categorized tabs
- **Mobile-First Navigation** with hamburger menu
- **SEO Optimized** with proper meta tags and Open Graph support
- **Fast Loading** – lightweight assets and optimized structure
- **Accessibility** focused (ARIA labels, semantic HTML)

## 📋 Sections

- **Top Bar** – Contact info & DOH license badge
- **Hero Section** – Strong value proposition with stats
- **Services** – Hematology, Clinical Chemistry, Microscopy, Immunology, Microbiology, Parasitology, and Phlebotomy
- **Why Choose Us** – Quality controls, EQA participation, and commitments
- **Test Menu** – Comprehensive searchable/categorized test list
- **About Us** – Mission, vision, and values
- **Our Team** – Medical technologists and staff
- **Patient Information** – Preparation guidelines and FAQs
- **Contact & Booking** – Form and location details
- **Footer** with quick links

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **HTML5**
- **CSS3** (Custom styles with CSS variables)
- **Vanilla JavaScript** (No heavy frameworks)
- **Google Fonts** (Cormorant Garant, Plus Jakarta Sans, DM Mono)
